Quiz 06: Basic Math Refresher
-----------------------------
- How can we add parenthesis to the following expression to make it equal 100?
    - 1 + 9 * 10
    - Answer: (1 + 9) * 10


- What is the result of the following expression?
    - 2 + 10 * 5 + 3 * 2
    - Answer: 58


- What is the result of the following expression?
    - 6 * 8 / 2**3 - 2 * 2
    - Answer: 2
