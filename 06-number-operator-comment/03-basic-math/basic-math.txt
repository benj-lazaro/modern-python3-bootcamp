Basic Math
----------
- Python's commonly used Math Operators
    - Addition              +
    - Subtraction           -
    - Multiplication        *
    - Division              /
    - Division (integer)    //
    - Exponentiation        **
    - Modulo                $


 - Python's "Order of Operations"
    - PEMDAS (highest to lowest)
        - Parenthesis
        - Exponentiation
        - Multiplication
        - Division
        - Addition
        - Subtraction
