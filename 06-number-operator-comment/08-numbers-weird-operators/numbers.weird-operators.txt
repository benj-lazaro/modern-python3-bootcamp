Numbers: Weird Operators
------------------------
- The exponentiation operator (**)
    - Takes two numbers
    - Raises a number (prefix) to a given power (suffix)


- The modulo operator (%)
    - Takes two numbers
    - Divides the numbers
    - Returns ONLY the remainder part

    - NOTE: Referred to as the remainder operator

    - Common use case
        - Figuring out if a number is even or odd


- The integer division (//)
    - Takes two numbers
    - Divides the numbers
    - It floors (i.e. rounds down) and returns ONLY the integer part
    - Omitting the decimal part
