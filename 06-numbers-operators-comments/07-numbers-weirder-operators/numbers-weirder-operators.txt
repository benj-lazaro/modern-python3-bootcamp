Weirder Operators
-----------------
- This refers to the math operators:
    - Exponentiation    **
    - Modulo            %
    - Integer Division  //

- Exponentiation raises a number to the given power

- Modulo also known as the Remainder Operator
    - Performs division
    - Returns the remainder instead
    - Often used in identifying if a number is even or odd

- Integer division performs a regular division
    - Returns a floored or rounded down result
    - To ensure the the result remains an integer
